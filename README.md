### 👨‍💻 About Me

With over **24 years of experience** in the IT industry, I am a seasoned Computer Programmer and Web Developer with a passion for clean code and problem-solving. I started my journey in 2000, mastering **PHP** and **SQL** to build bespoke database-driven applications.

Recently, I have pivoted deeply into **Artificial Intelligence**, utilizing tools like **Amazon Q**, **OpenAI**, and **n8n** to build "Agentic" AI tools and automation workflows. Whether it's maintaining complex legacy CMS platforms or "vibe coding" the next generation of AI apps, I love building things that work.

* 
**Specializing in Local LLM Implementation, Agentic Workflows, and Game Logic.**

I build bridge-systems that connect traditional software architecture with modern Artificial Intelligence. Beyond my public contributions, I have extensive experience building **private, production-ready AI agents** and **automated trading systems** using Python, PyTorch, and local-first LLM orchestration (OpenAI API, Openrouter.ai, and Ollama).
